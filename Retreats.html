Retreats

Come away with us.

All of our cabins are fitted with the necessities and simple comforts of home. A full kitchen, bathroom with shower and tub, and cozy living room await.

Walter's Cabin

Beginner Level Member

Named after the original farmer who settled this land, Walter's cabin is next to our entry road. There are two bedrooms, one with a king-sized bed and the other has two twin beds. In the living room, the couch pulls out to a queen sleeper. Walter's Cabin sleep 6.

>

Honeysuckle Cabin

Tucked into our woods is the Honeysuckle cabin. In the spring honeysuckle grows and scents the air as you towards the door. This cabin has one bedroom and one loft bed and sleeps 4.

>

Shepherd's Rest Cabin

Adjacent to our sheep pen, you will find the Shepherd's Rest Cabin. Wake and spend the morning on the front porch, watching the birds and listening to the gentle sounds of the farm. This cabin has one bedroom with a king-sized bed and fits two.
