About Us

Bluebird Acres started in 2015, when we were looking for a way to slow down and taste the good life. We started with a small 10 acre plot and over time were able to expand to now over 150 acres. Our goals are simple: provide a place of health and nourishment the encompasses all aspects of our humanity and to nurture connection to the earth. From there, we continued to grow the diversity within our farm. Later we were able to add cabins on our property so that our CSA members could have a place to come away and refresh.
